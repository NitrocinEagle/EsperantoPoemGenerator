Welcome to the esperanto poem generator page. Here you can generate some poem on esperanto just ckicked on the button "Generate!". Also you are able to add words to the dictonary and templates. Yuo kust need to add it to these files:

  • "Templates.json"
  • "Dictonary_Verbs.json"
  • "Dictonary_Nouns.json"
  • "Dictonary_Adjectives.json"

This makes random poems. First, it randomly selects sentence patterns. Then, wherever the pattern has a number, it randomly selects a word from one of the numbered word lists. You can either choose one of the sample sets of words and sentence patterns, or you can enter your own words and sentence patterns.
